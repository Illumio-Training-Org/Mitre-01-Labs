Key Concepts to Remember

Lateral Movement (TA0008) is the universal chokepoint — blast radius is determined by how far an attacker moves before detection. Illumio Insights is strongest here.
Risky Services Traffic → TA0008, External Data Transfer → TA0010, Malicious IP Traffic → TA0011. These three views are the core Insights detection layer.
T1021.002 (SMB, port 445) was the lateral movement mechanism for WannaCry and NotPetya via EternalBlue (MS17-010). It remains actively exploited today.
Illumio Insights operates primarily at the IOA level — detecting behavioral anomalies, not just known-bad signatures. This means it can surface novel attacks that haven't been seen before.
Nation-state APTs — APT28 (Russia/GRU), APT41 (China), APT39 (Iran/IRGC), Lazarus Group (DPRK) — all use Lateral Movement extensively in documented campaigns.
